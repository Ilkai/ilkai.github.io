About

Hi, my name is Lynden, and I'm a UX & UI Designer from Melbourne, Victoria.

I've been designing digital products for web and native for over four years. I have developed a host of versatile skills, including thorough functional and non-functional requirements gathering, distillation of requirements into an information architecture and site maps, designing and testing potential solutions, and leading implementation within small Agile development teams.

By virtue of my natural curiosity for tech, I've taught myself Git and Git Flow, can use a unix-style CLI, write semantic HTML, and style my own visual designs in CSS flawlessly. I've also created interaction and motion prototypes using Framer, Pixate (RIP), Form (RIP), Origami Studio, Codepen and Principle.

I'm also a client-facing project lead and bring my enthusiasm for elegant design into every meeting. I'm able to quickly build rapport through transparent, open communication and I actively cultivate and value collaborative and trusting professional relationships.

Please don't hesitate to reach out on Dribbble or LinkedIn if I would be a great addition to your team or project.
