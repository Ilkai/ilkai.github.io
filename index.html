About

I have been gathering requirements, reviewing analytics reports, flow charting, user testing and pushing pixels since 2014.

I'm a detail-oriented designer (read: pixel-perfect, OCD kinda thing), currently helping our clients build great mobile apps at Domestic Cat Software. Typically, what has to be built and what it does at a broad level has already been defined by the client. I set out to materialise it by distilling the requirements into an information architecture, flow diagrams, clickable prototypes, and production-ready designs.

My technical understanding of the technology that unpins what I design is one of my biggest strengths. I find that the more I understand the technical environment I'm working in, the easier I can craft flows that don't end up costing months of development time. I also just generally enjoy reading about tech, especially around native mobile.

I worked at Kogan.com as a UI/UX designer, designing and developing the checkout, EDM templates, and designing mobile apps. I also cut my teeth on CSS, including LESS and SCSS.

I’m currently working at Domestic Cat Software, managing native mobile app projects, the odd web project, and leading design in general. I’ve had the opportunity to work with companies big and small, from Coles, DELWP and Hungry Jacks, to ChimpChange, Palette and T Garage.
