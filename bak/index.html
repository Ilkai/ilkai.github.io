Work

More to Explore

iOS app for DELWP

More to Explore is an app to find the many recreational activities and visitor sites across Victoria.

I was responsible for leading the project, including being the main point of contact for the DELWP product owner. I was also responsible for all of the UX and UI design, a small set of custom icons, and managing our development team.

Unnamed Digital Bank

iOS app

Our design team was engaged to help a company design a sister-app, backed by their existing banking platform.

We went to the drawing board to design the information architecture from the ground up, rearranging and redesigning existing features where neccessary and adding a few new ones. We ran quantitative surveys, produced a set of app architecture diagrams and wireframes, user tested critical flow prototypes and developed some visual design directions.

Insight IQ

iOS & Android apps

We helped our client redesign their existing market research community platform for iOS and Android native apps, with a secondary goal of an app that could be rebranded and licensed to other companies.

I ran workshops with stakeholders, produced wireframe prototypes, and managed the development team across both platforms.
